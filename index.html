Frequently Asked Questions

At TAJS Furniture, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service and ensuring a smooth shopping experience. To help you find quick answers to common questions, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions. If you don't find the information you're looking for, please feel free to contact our customer service team.

1. What materials are used in your furniture?

Our furniture is crafted from high-quality materials, including solid hardwoods, premium fabrics, and durable leathers. Each piece is designed to ensure longevity and aesthetic appeal.

2. Do you offer customization options for your furniture?

Yes, we offer customization options for many of our furniture pieces. You can choose from various finishes, fabrics, and configurations to suit your personal style and space requirements.

3. Do you offer warranties on your furniture?

Yes, we offer warranties on many of our furniture pieces. The warranty period and coverage vary depending on the item. Please check the product details or contact our customer service team for more information.

4. Can I visit your showroom?

Absolutely! We welcome you to visit our showroom to see our furniture collection in person. Our knowledgeable staff will be happy to assist you with any questions and help you find the perfect pieces for your home.

5. What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your furniture, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided it is in its original condition. Please refer to our return policy page for more details.
